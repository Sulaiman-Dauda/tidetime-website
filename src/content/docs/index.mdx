## Up and running in one command

No `.env` to edit, no glue to write. The installer checks for Docker, generates
secure secrets, launches the app and database, runs migrations, and waits until
everything is healthy.

## Everything a scheduling platform should have

<p class="tt-lead">From a single consultant link to a full team operation — Tidetime scales with you.</p>

<div class="tt-grid">
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">📅</span>
    <h3>Booking pages</h3>
    <p>Shareable personal and team pages with your branding, custom questions, durations, buffers, and notice rules.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">👥</span>
    <h3>Team scheduling</h3>
    <p>Round-robin, collective, and multi-attendant assignment with role-based access for your whole team.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">🔄</span>
    <h3>Calendar sync</h3>
    <p>Two-way sync with Google Calendar, Microsoft 365 / Outlook, and Apple / CalDAV. No double-bookings.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">🎥</span>
    <h3>Automatic meeting links</h3>
    <p>Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Daily, or built-in Jitsi — minted per booking, no manual steps.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">💳</span>
    <h3>Payments &amp; deposits</h3>
    <p>Collect full payment or a deposit at booking time with Stripe. Confirmations and refunds handled for you.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">🗳️</span>
    <h3>Meeting polls</h3>
    <p>Propose several times, let invitees vote, and book the winning slot — with flexible result visibility.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">🧭</span>
    <h3>Routing forms</h3>
    <p>Ask a few questions, then send each person to the right service, an external link, or a message.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">⭐</span>
    <h3>Reviews</h3>
    <p>Collect private feedback and route happy customers to your public review page above a star threshold.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="tt-card">
    <span class="tt-ico">🔔</span>
    <h3>Reminders &amp; emails</h3>
    <p>Branded confirmations, reschedules, cancellations, and timed reminders over your own SMTP.</p>
  </div>
</div>

</div>
