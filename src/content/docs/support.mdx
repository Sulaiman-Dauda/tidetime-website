Tidetime is **free, open-source, and self-funded**. There's no paid tier gating
features, no venture money, and no data resold on the side — just software that
respects the people who run it. If Tidetime saves you time or money, here are the
ways you can help keep it healthy and moving forward.

## Fund the work

Sponsorship pays for the unglamorous parts — security updates, dependency
upgrades, documentation, and the time to review contributions. Even a small
recurring amount makes a real difference.

## Where your support goes

Every contribution goes straight back into the project:

- **Security** — timely patches, dependency upgrades, and audits.
- **Reliability** — fixing bugs, improving tests, and keeping deployments smooth.
- **Documentation** — the guides on this site, kept accurate and current.
- **New features** — building the most-requested capabilities, in the open.

Thank you for being part of it. Tidetime exists because people like you choose to
support open software. 🌊
